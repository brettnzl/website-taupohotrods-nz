Tokoroa Westpac Charity Run

This was held at Tokoroa with a run to amilton Hospital where we were shown around the Westpac helicopter hangar.
Many cars and bikes attended and included a fun prizegiving and auction night at the Tokoroa Club.
Pete and Debbie Stevens won Best car with Christine.
